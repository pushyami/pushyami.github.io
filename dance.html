Dancing

Since childhood I had innate passion for dance. This passion grew and I continued performing at my university too, where I was the cultural head for a community called "Vayu". I am a trained Kuchipudi Dancer. I have participated in every intra collegiate cultural event and won many laurels. I choreograph all of my the performances and teach it to others too.

Awards

  1. Stood first in "Classical Dance-Group" category in IIIT H for four consecutive years.
  2. Performed an award winning performance of a Bharatanatyam Dance ballet of "Dasavatar" in my freshman year.
  3. Songs choreographed by me won second and thrid prizes in the years 2013 and 2015 respectively.

Jury

  1. Was invited as a distinguished member of jury to judge the "Freshers" Program, one of the largest events in IIIT Hyderabad for the year 2016.
  2. Was invited to judge an annual cultural event of IIIT Hyderabad.
