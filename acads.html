Bachelor's of Technology

Selection into IIIT is a highly competetive process. The selection required a preliminary "All India Engineering Exntrance Examination", upon qualification, I topped the aptitude and objective exam conducted by IIIT-H. I was one of the 500 students shortlisted for interviews. I aced the interviews and I was one of the top 1% of 1.2 million aspirants to join IIIT Hyderabad, a university known for promoting undergraduate research.

I have done my undergraduation at IIITH from August 2011 to May 2015. I majored in Computer Science and I have succesfully completed by Bachelor's with a GPA of 7.82/10. I am interested in Computer Vision, particularly Computational Photography, Image and Video Analysis and 3D reconstruction.

In my sophomore and Junior year, I started participating in workshops around the country, in order to gain some perspective on how the work is done outside of my college and to keep myself updated constantly. It was during these workshops, I started thinking about research career seriously.
